Tutorial : Process Discovery

  

Objective :

Discover event driven processes.

Expected Duration : 5 Minutes.

Context :

Before a process written in Apex can be referenced as a step in a sequence, it must first be discovered and registered in the Event Driven framework.
The automated mechanism for doing this is initiated by clicking the button.

Detailed Steps :

Click the Application icon on the top right of the screen.

Click the tab and Click on the list view to reveal the list view of the currently installed processes.

Click the button.

The refresh process can take up to a couple of minutes - progress is shown at the top of the page as follows: .

The framework analyses apex logic available to the org to discover all processes conforming to the event driven framework specification.
All valid EDA processes are registered and made available for potential selection by the system orchestrator when building sequences under the tab.
