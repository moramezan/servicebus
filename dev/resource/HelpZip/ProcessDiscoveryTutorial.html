Tutorial : Process Discovery

 

Objective :

Discover ESB processes.

Expected Duration : 5 Minutes.

Context :

Before an ESB process can be referenced as a step in a sequence, it must first be discovered and registered in the ESB framework.
The automated mechanism for doing this is initiated by clicking the button.

Detailed Steps :

Click the Application icon on the top right of the screen.

Click the tab and Click on the list view to reveal the list view of the currently installed processes.

Click the button.

The refresh process can range in duration from a few seconds up to a few minutes depending on the availability of Salesforce servers
Progress is shown at the top of the page as follows: .

The ESB framework analyses all apex logic available to the org to discover processes conforming to the ESB framework specification.
All valid ESB processes are registered and made available for potential selection by the system orchestrator when building sequences under the tab.
