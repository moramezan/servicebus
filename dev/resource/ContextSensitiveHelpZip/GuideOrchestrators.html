Orchestrators Guide

Intended Audience: Configurator / Business Analyst / Salesforce System Administrator

About This Guide

This guide is intended for Business Analysts and System Administrators who primarily make use of point and click actions to operate, configure, and maintain services. The purpose of this guide is to

  • Explain basic messaging as well as communication concepts and terminology.
  • Describe the use of point and click as a tool to configure services.
  • Describe the use use point and click to operate and monitor the Services framework.

This guide does not cover designing, building and deploying custom services. This topic is described in the separate Service Developer's Guide.
