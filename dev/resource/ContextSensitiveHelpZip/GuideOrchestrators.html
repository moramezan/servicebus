Orchestrators Guide

Intended Audience: Configurator / Business Analyst / Salesforce System Administrator

About This Guide

This guide is intended for Business Analysts and System Administrators who primarily make use of point and click actions to operate, configure, and maintain services. The purpose of this guide is to

  • Explain basic messaging and communication concepts and terminology.
  • Describe how to use point and click as a tool to configure services.
  • Describe how to use use point and click to operate and monitor the Service Bus.

This guide does not cover designing building and deploying custom services. This topic is described in the separate Process Developer's Guide.
