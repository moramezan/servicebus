Core Processes

This section is intended for Service Bus Configurators, Business Analysts, Salesforce System Administrators, and Force.com Apex Developers. The purpose is to explain the use and configuration of each pre-packaged process in the Service Bus framework.

This section does not cover designing building and deploying custom services. That topic is described in the separate Process Developer's Guide. This section does not cover configuring or monitoring the Enterprise Service Bus. That topic is described in the separate Orchestrator's Guide.

The Service Bus has pre-packaged services. These pre-packaged processes are classified under the tag "Service Bus". Some processes work in isolation (for example Wiretap) while other processes work in tandem (for example Publish and Subscribe)

Note Some processes are no-operation or pass-through in nature. These simply forward all received messages to the next step in their respective sequence, without any side effects.
