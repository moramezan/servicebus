Process Discovery Tutorial

The objective of this tutorial is to discover new processes so they can be referenced by a step in a sequence.

Expected duration: 5 minutes.

Instructions

Before a Process can be referenced as a step in a sequence, it must first be discovered and registered by the ESB framework. The framework can analyse all Apex logic in the organization to discover classes that conform to a process specification. All valid ESB processes are registered and made available for potential selection by the system orchestrator when building sequences under the tab.

  1. Open the App menu on the top right of Salesforce and choose Event Driven Architecture.
  2. Navigate to the Processes tab, choose the All Processes and click Go. This will take you to a list view where you can see the Discover button.

  3. Click the Discover button. This initiates the automated mechanism that finds new processes and refreshes existing ones.

    Note Discovery can range in duration from a few seconds to a few minutes depending on the availability of Salesforce servers. Progress is shown at the top of the page.

  4. Once discovery is complete, you will be taken back to the list view.
